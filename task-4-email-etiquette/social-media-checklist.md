# Social Media Do's and Don'ts 

## ✔️ Do’s(What you should do)

•	Follow pages and people that add value (learning, networking, opportunities). 

• Always acknowledge sources when sharing content or ideas. 

•	Set boundaries to avoid distraction from academics and real-life interactions. 

•	Build a profile (e.g., projects, internships, achievements). 

•	Maintain a polite tone, even during disagreements. 

## ❌ Don’ts(What you should not do)

•	Don’t use social media during important academic time.

•	Don’t post content that could affect future opportunities.

•	Don’t trust unknown links or profiles

•	Avoid posting sensitive data like address, phone number, or schedules.

•	Don’t ignore digital wellbeing
